Easy White Chicken Chili

A delicious bowl of White Chicken Chili

An easy to make chili on the lighter side o fhte color pallate. A great chili for when it gets chilly.

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Chicken
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Broth
  • Beans
  • Green Chiles
  • Seasonings
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook chicken, onion, and garlic in hot oil until chicken is brwned on both sides.
  2. Cut the chicken into pieces, then return it to the Dutch oven.
  3. Add broth, beans, chiles, oregano, cumin, and ceyenne pepper.
  4. Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook until the chicken is cooked through.
  5. Divide cilanro among the bowls, ladle chili over cilantro, and top with cheese.
